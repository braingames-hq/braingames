Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

Introduction

Brain Games ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Brain Games handles information when you use our iOS puzzle game application.

In short: Brain Games does not collect any personal information. All game data stays on your device. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

Data Collection

What We Collect

Brain Games collects no data whatsoever. We do not use any analytics services, crash reporting tools, or tracking technologies.

What We Do NOT Collect

Brain Games does NOT collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Device identifiers or advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Location data
  • Contact lists or calendar data
  • Crash logs or performance metrics
  • Any information that can identify you personally

Brain Games is a fully offline application. No data ever leaves your device.

Local Data Storage

Brain Games stores the following data locally on your device using iOS storage (AsyncStorage):

  • Game Progress - Your current level, scores, and puzzle states for each game
  • Purchase Status - Whether you have purchased the full version (stored locally for instant access)
  • Game Preferences - Difficulty settings and game configuration

All data is stored exclusively on your device. Nothing is transmitted to external servers, cloud services, or third parties.

In-App Purchases

Brain Games offers a one-time, non-consumable in-app purchase to unlock the full game. This purchase is processed entirely by Apple's App Store infrastructure.

  • We do not process, store, or have access to your payment information
  • All billing is handled by Apple through your Apple ID
  • Purchase receipts are validated locally on your device via Apple's StoreKit framework
  • We do not operate any backend servers for purchase verification

For information about how Apple handles your purchase data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

Apple App Store

Brain Games is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect data as described in their privacy policy. We have no access to this data.

What We Do NOT Use

Brain Games does NOT use:

  • Analytics platforms (no Firebase, PostHog, Amplitude, etc.)
  • Crash reporting services (no Crashlytics, Sentry, etc.)
  • Advertising networks or ad tracking
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud storage services
  • Data brokers or data selling services

We will never sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for any purpose.

Network Connectivity

Brain Games requires no internet connection to function. The only network activity occurs when:

  • Making a purchase - Apple's StoreKit connects to the App Store to process your payment
  • Restoring a purchase - Apple's StoreKit connects to verify previous purchases

All gameplay is 100% offline. You can play Sudoku, PathFinder, and Maze without any internet connection.

Children's Privacy

Brain Games is safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information from any user, we are in full compliance with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and similar international regulations.

Parents and guardians can feel confident that their children's privacy is protected when using Brain Games.

Data Security

Your data security is ensured through our privacy-first architecture:

  • All game data is stored locally on your device
  • No data transmission means no risk of interception
  • Your game progress is protected by iOS's security model
  • No server infrastructure means no data breach risk

We recommend keeping your device secure with a passcode and ensuring iOS is up to date.

Your Rights and Controls

You have complete control over your data:

  • Data Deletion - Delete all app data by deleting Brain Games from your device
  • Purchase Restoration - Restore your purchase on any device using your Apple ID
  • No Account Required - No sign-up, login, or account creation needed to use the app

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Continued use of Brain Games after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
