Our Games

Sudoku

The classic number puzzle. Fill a 9x9 grid so every row, column, and 3x3 box contains digits 1-9. Four difficulty levels from easy to expert, with notes mode, undo support, and mistake tracking.

PathFinder

Find the one path that visits every cell exactly once. Choose the correct starting cell and follow the arrows through the grid. Progressive difficulty with increasingly complex layouts.

Maze

Navigate through procedurally generated mazes to reach the goal. Swipe to move through square, circular, diamond, and cross-shaped mazes that grow more complex as you progress.
